About Me.

I'm Kasweka Michael Mukoko — alias wababaDev. Software engineer based in Lusaka, Zambia. My professional background is in fintech and payments infrastructure, but right now I'm in a phase of deliberately going wider: revisiting CS fundamentals I glossed over the first time, exploring data and analytics, and building things that solve real problems for real people in contexts like Zambia.

Currently the sole developer at a financial services company — which means I own the full stack, from backend systems and internal tooling to infrastructure and deployment. I find I do my best work when I understand why something works, not just that it does.

Away from the keyboard: I collect Rubik's cubes (6 and counting), play guitar, get lost in sci-fi and classic cinema, and have an anime backlog I keep telling myself I'll get to. Currently playing Hollow Knight. Dream: one day fusing amapiano, jazz and orchestra into something no one asked for but everyone will need.
